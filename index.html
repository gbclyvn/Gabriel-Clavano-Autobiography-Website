I was born on November 25, 2006 in Santa Maria, Bulacan. It was a small, quiet place and I have good memories from when I was a kid there. But when I was 8 or 9, my family moved to Iligan City. Inspiration üí°: My Greatest Mentor

Thomas Edison

Thomas Edison made the light bulb. He didn't just get it right the first time. He tried and failed 1,000 times before it worked. He said that success is 1% having a good idea and 99% doing the hard work. This means you have to work really hard and keep trying even when you fail a lot.

What I Learned: Every time I fail a project, every time someone says no to me, every bug in my code is not bad. It's just information that helps me learn. It's the hard work, the late nights coding, the doing the same thing over and over, the discipline of showing up every single day that makes dreams come true. There are no shortcuts to anything worth having.
